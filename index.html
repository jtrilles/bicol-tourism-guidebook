About

This guidebook provides a comprehensive strategic framework for transforming Bicol Region into a world-class tourism destination. It covers market analysis, global benchmarking, digital strategy, six-province development plans, sustainability frameworks, and a phased execution roadmap through 2028.

Contents

  • Part I: Market Study and Global Benchmarking — 15 destinations, provincial SWOTs, financial projections
  • Part II: Digital Marketing and Social Media Strategy — content playbook, platform guides, influencer model
  • Part III: Tourism Development Plan — province plans, air access, circuits, events, niche products
  • Part IV: Destination Management — sustainability, quality standards, crisis management, CBT
  • Part V: Execution — budget, KPIs, roadmap, case studies, ASEAN compliance
  • Appendices: Investment profiles, training curriculum, environmental guidelines, templates

Data Sources

Philippine Statistics Authority, ASEAN Secretariat, United Nations World Tourism Organization, World Travel and Tourism Council, and international destination marketing organizations.
