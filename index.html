About the Guidebook

This comprehensive 170+ page guidebook provides a data-driven strategic framework for transforming Bicol Region into a world-class tourism destination. It covers market analysis, global benchmarking across 15 destinations, digital marketing strategy, province-by-province development plans, sustainability frameworks, and a phased execution roadmap through 2028.
