About the Guidebook

This guidebook provides a comprehensive strategic framework for transforming the Bicol Region into a world-class tourism destination. It covers market analysis, global benchmarking across 15 destinations, digital marketing strategy, six-province development plans, sustainability frameworks, and a phased execution roadmap through 2028.

Contents

  • Part I: Market Study and Global Benchmarking — 15 destinations compared
  • Part II: Digital Marketing and Social Media Strategy — 1M follower growth engine
  • Part III: Tourism Development Plan — 6 provinces, air access, circuits, events
  • Part IV: Destination Management — sustainability, quality, crisis, CBT
  • Part V: Execution — budget, KPIs, roadmap, ASEAN compliance
  • Appendices A-K: Investment profiles, training curriculum, templates
